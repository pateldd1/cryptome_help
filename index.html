CryptoMe Help Page

Depositing XRP into your CryptoMe wallet

Create or import an XRP wallet to deposit XRP directly

  1. Press to create or import a wallet. Importing a wallet requires your XRP secret key.
  2. Your secret key is copied to the clipboard. PASTE THIS IN NOTES OR WRITE IT DOWN. This is how to regain access to your wallet or import your wallet into any other XRP Wallet app.
  3. This is your QR code for depositing XRP.
  4. After pressing clipboard button, your XRP address is copied. Go to Coinbase or wherever you own XRP and deposit to this address. XRP wallets require 10 XRP minimum to activate. You must maintain 10 XRP in your wallet.
  5. After pressing the settings button, you'll see the options for removing your wallet or seeing your secret key. Press the small QR button to return to the QR page.

Depositing other Cryptos as XRP into your CryptoMe Wallet

  1. Press the coin's bank button to select the coin you want to deposit as XRP.Press the coin's bank button to select the coin you want to deposit as XRP.
  2. Receiving from Atom address and memo in case Changelly Deposit fails. It will get refunded back to this address and memo.
  3. Go to Coinbase or wherever you own Atom and deposit to this address.
  4. Copy the deposit address and memo. This is the Changelly return address in case Changelly fails.
  5. Fill in the address and memo in CryptoMe. Must send more than the minimum required. I would suggest sending 10% more than the minimum to avoid any minimum send errors.
  6. The app will show you a Changelly deposit Atom address and memo which will be filled out in Coinbase.
  7. Send the Atom from Coinbase to the Changelly deposit address and memo.
  8. Keep track of your transaction in Coinbase.
  9. After the transaction is complete, you'll see the conversion in CryptoMe.
  10. Press the conversion you're interested in. Here we see the status as waiting.
  11. After the conversion is complete, you'll see the XRP in your wallet.
  12. Press the conversion again and you should see the status as finished.

Send XRP to user

Sending XRP or equivalent USD to another user

  1. Search for a user by their screen name.
  2. After you've searched a user, you can send them XRP or USD equivalent. Input a description for your transaction.
  3. After you've searched a user, you can view all the transactions you've made with them after pressing the transactions button.

Send to XRP Address

Sending XRP or equivalent USD to another XRP address

  1. Select the paper plane icon next to XRP.
  2. Input the XRP address and destination tag (if required) you want to send to. Input the amount in XRP and equivalent USD. Then press the pay button to pay.

Send XRP to 80+ other coins

  1. Search for the coin you want to convert to. Here we're converting to Solana.
  2. Go to your Solana wallet. Here we're using Phantom Wallet.
  3. Copy your Solana wallet address.
  4. Input your Solana wallet address in CryptoMe.
  5. After pressing convert, you'll see the conversion page.
  6. After pressing pay, you'll see the payment sent for conversion.
  7. After the conversion order is placed, you'll see the conversion order in CryptoMe.
  8. Press the conversion order you're interested in. Here we see the status as confirming. Drag down to refresh this page to keep track of the conversion.
  9. After the conversion is complete, you'll see the SOL in your Phantom Wallet.
  10. See the transaction in your Phantom Wallet.
