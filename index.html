CryptoMe Help Section

Depositing XRP into your CryptoMe wallet

Press to create or import a wallet. Importing a wallet requires your XRP secret key.

Your secret key is copied to the clipboard. PASTE THIS IN NOTES OR WRITE IT DOWN. This is how to regain access to your wallet or import your wallet into any other XRP Wallet app

This is your QR code for depositing XRP.

After pressing clipboard button, your XRP address is copied. Go to Coinbase or wherever you own XRP and deposit to this address. XRP wallets require 10 XRP minimum to activate. You must maintain 10 XRP in your wallet.

After pressing the settings button, you'll see the options for removing your wallet or seeing your secret key. Press the small QR button to return to the QR page.

Depositing 150+ other Cryptos as XRP into your CryptoMe Wallet (For ExperiencedCrypto Users)

Press the coin's bank button to select the coin you want to deposit as XRP.

Receiving from Atom address and memo in case Changelly Deposit fails. It will get refunded back to this address

Go to Coinbase or wherever you own Atom and deposit to this address.

Copy the deposit address and memo. This is the Changelly return address in case Changelly fails.

Fill in the address and memo in CryptoMe. Must send more than the minimum required. I would suggest sending 10% more than the minimum to avoid any minimum send errors.

The app will show you a Changelly deposit Atom address and memo which will be filled out in Coinbase.

Send the Atom from Coinbase to the Changelly deposit address and memo.

Keep track of your transaction in Coinbase.

After the transaction is complete, you'll see the conversion in CryptoMe.

Press the conversion you're interested in. Here we see the status as waiting.

After the conversion is complete, you'll see the XRP in your wallet.

Press the conversion again and you should see the status as finished.

Pay another CryptoMe User in XRP (P2P Transaction)

Search for a user by their screen name.

After you've searched a user, you can send them XRP or USD equivalent. Input a description for your transaction.

After you've searched a user, you can view all the transactions you've made with them after pressing the transactions button.

Withdraw to XRP Address

Select the paper plane icon next to XRP.

Input the XRP address and destination tag (if required) you want to send to. Input the amount in XRP and equivalent USD. Then press the pay button to pay. For example, you can withdraw to Coinbase this way to withdraw USD from there.

Withdraw and Convert XRP to 150+ other cryptocurrencies (For Experienced Crypto Users)

Search for the coin you want to convert to. Here we're converting to Solana.

Go to your Solana wallet. Here we're using Phantom Wallet.

Copy your Solana wallet address.

Input your Solana wallet address in CryptoMe.

After pressing convert, you'll see the conversion page.

After pressing pay, you'll see the payment sent for conversion.

After the conversion order is placed, you'll see the conversion order in CryptoMe.

Press the conversion order you're interested in. Here we see the status as confirming. Drag down to refresh this page to keep track of the conversion.

After the conversion is complete, you'll see the SOL in your Phantom Wallet.

See the transaction in your Phantom Wallet.
