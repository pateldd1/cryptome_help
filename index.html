CryptoMe Help Page

Depositing XRP into your CryptoMe wallet

1. Create or import an xrp wallet

  • You can create a wallet or import one. To import one, you must have the secret key of the wallet you're trying to import. Dont worry we don't store your secret key on our side. It's only on your device.

2. Deposit XRP into your account by converting from 30+ cryptocurrencies into XRP. A minimum of 10 XRP is required in any new ripple wallet

  • The bank button will deposit that cryptocurrency as XRP to your CryptoMe wallet. The paper airplane button allows converting your XRP to the cryptocurrency you're trying to receive in your other wallet. e.g. coinbase wallet if you want to receive bitcoin in that wallet.

  • Following Left Image: As an example, if you wanted to send 1 BTC from your coinbase account into CryptoMe, you would press the bank button next to BTC on the exchange and would be sent to the following page. Enter 1 BTC and the corresponding amount of XRP that will be deposited to your CryptoMe wallet will be shown. Please enter the BTC wallet address from Coinbase as a safety measure in case this transaction does not go through so your BTC will be returned to that wallet in Coinbase.

  • Following Right Image: On the following page with the QR code, you receive instructions. Send 1 BTC to 33ss6k9jeHEC91NujC….. BTC address, which is a Changelly Address, a service that provides cryptocurrency conversions. In this case, your wallet address would be raa6pj…. 1 BTC sent from your Coinbase account to the Changelly address would deposit 46633.4857 XRP to your XRP address in CryptoMe

  • To deposit xrp from another xrp wallet, simply send to your xrp wallet in CryptoMe.

  • Go to the home page and into "orders" to see the status of your exchange transaction

  • Once this transaction is complete, you will have the amount of xrp signified by the cryptocurrency conversion order. In this case, 47213.879 XRP would be deposited into your CryptoMe wallet

Sending

Sending XRP or equivalent $ to another user

  • In this case $10 or 48.86 XRP would be sent to the other user.

Send XRP to another XRP address

Send BTC or one of 30 other cryptocurrencies to another BTC/other crypto wallet

  • Simply Enter the amount of XRP or other coin you would like to send to a wallet that can hold that coin

  • Press the withdraw button. In this case, 10000 XRP would be sent as 0.2115 to the receiving bitcoin wallet

  • You can view the status of this order in the "orders" section as well like above.
