Depositing XRP into your CryptoMe wallet

1. Create or import an XRP wallet

  • You can create a wallet or import one. To import one, you must have the secret key of the wallet you're trying to import. Don't worry, we don't store your secret key on our side. It's only on your device.
  • To deposit XRP into CryptoMe from another XRP wallet like from Coinbase, simply send it to your XRP wallet in CryptoMe.

Send XRP to user

Sending XRP or equivalent USD to another user

  • In this case $10 or 48.86 XRP would be sent to the other user's XRP address and account.

Send to XRP Address

Sending XRP or equivalent USD to another XRP address

  • In this case $10 or 48.86 XRP would be sent to another XRP address.
